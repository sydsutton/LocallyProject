About Locally

Locally was born inside the mind of a crazed maniac living in a post-apocalyptic-looking warehouse. During COVID-19, the music scene in his city was reduced to ash, and with it, his love for music. But, with his help, his local music scene, as well as the music scene of literally everywhere on planet Earth, will rise from the ashes like the phoenix it is. Put your party pants on, ladies and gentlemen-- it's about to get weird.
